About My Birthdays

I was born in Yokosuka, Japan on December 27th. Being 2 days after christmas, most things are closed so most events we did in Janurary. Personally I like having a december birthday, christmas is my favorite time of year so having a birthday right after makes it more enjoyable. It's also much easier to think about when thinking back concerning birthdays because its right at the end of the year.

For some reason I get asked a lot if I get less presents because its right after christmas, but my family always just waited 2 days and then gave me a birthday gift as well. I also get asked a lot if I know japanese since I was born in Japan, but unfortunatley I do not.
