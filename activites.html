Refreshments & Activites Party

My family generally follows a pretty standard birthday personalized to each person

The Dinner

Whoever's birthday it is decides dinner for that night, typically its Pizza or Japanese for me. Due to this we usually end up at a buffet for pizza or a fancy Japanese resturaunt with Sukiyaki.


The Dessert

The birthday person also decides the type of cake or dessert for the night as well. Personally I dont like cake so I usually always choose Cheesecake or one of those giant cookie pies from bakeries.


Drinks

I'm a big fan of EggNogg and its only sold once a year, so every birthday a gallon or two has to be bought.


Bowling

My favorite thing to do for birthdays is to usually go Bowling with the family or friends. So often times we visit a bowling alley and spend an hour or two there


Movies

December often has some good movie showings, so if a good movie was released that year we often visted the movies as well.


Presents

Presents are usually immedietly after or during dessert for my family. Often times though people will get excited and gift early.
